Bass guitar

Bass is the instrument that support the sonic link between the rhythmical and melodic elements of any band.



The bass guitar, electric bass , or simply bass, is the lowest-pitched member of the guitar family. is a plucked string instrument similar

in appearance and construction to an electric or an acoustic guitar, but with a longer neck and scale length, and typically four to six strings

or courses. Since the mid-1950s, the bass guitar has largely replaced the double bass in popular music.
